About Me

I guess you can call it a resume

Here's the thing...

I've had a goal since High School to never submit a single resume. Up to this point I've tried the following enterprises:

  • Landscaper
  • Bus Driver
  • Heavy equipment operator
  • Mechanic/Fabricator
  • Marketing Strategy Consultant
  • Podcaster
  • Seminary Teacher
So far no resume required.

So in keeping with that goal, I've decided to make up 3 different categories to demonstrate my coding skills

  1. Most Life Changing Books
  2. What I'll do with $1M
  3. Quotes to live by

Most Life Changing Books

  1. "The Book of Mormon" Another Testament of Jesus Christ
    • A historical account of Jesus Christ and his dealings with an ancient American Civilization
  2. "The Promise of a Pencil" By Adam Braun
    • An amazing road map for chasing a crazy dream and a meaningful life
  3. "Into the Magic Shop" By Dr. James R Doty
    • A fascinating Auto-Biography of a heart surgeon and how he learned to unify his heart and mind
  4. "How Will You Measure Your Life?" By Clayton Christensen
    • A powerful book about the theory of designing, living and leaving behind a life well lived

What I'll do with $1M

This one's pretty simple. It should take a lot less than a million bucks so maybe a better name would be, "My ideal rich life"

The first thing I'll do is buy a little float plane. I'll finish up with what I have left to earn my private pilot license. Then I'll build a big huge spacious log cabin on the banks of some lake way the flip up in Canada. Then I'll spend all my summers flying to our lakeside cabin.
